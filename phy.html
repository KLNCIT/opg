All Districts

Concerned Department Welfare of Differently Abled Persons
Concerned District All District
Organization Name welfare of differentliy abled department Scheme Details
Title /name APPOINTMENT OF GUARDIANS TO SPECIAL CATEGORIES OF DIFFERENTLY ABLED PERSONS UNDER NATIONAL TRUST ACT, 1999
Sponsored By State
Beneficiaries Physically Challenged [Differently abled]
Benefits Types Education Benefits
Other Details Should be affected with any one of the disability of Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Mentaly Retardation and Multiple disabilities.
How to avail District Collector and Local Level Committee, Chairman (National Trust Act) or Office of District Differently Abled Welfare Officer Grievance if any to be addressed to - Chairman, National Trust, New Delhi.
Description The persons affected with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, mental retardation and multiple disability who have crossed 18 years of age who are not in a position to take any decision. Hence under National Trust Act 1999 a Guardian can be appointed to look after their welfare, after the demise of their parents.

Title /name BEHIND THE EAR HEARING AID
Sponsored By State
Beneficiaries Physically Challenged [Differently abled]
Benefits Types Grants
Other Details Students studying 6 th Std. and above.
How to avail District Differently Abled Welfare Officer.Grievances if any to be addressed to Principal Secretary / State Commissioner for the Differently Abled, State Resource cum Training Centre Campus, Jawaharlal Nehru Inner Ring Road, K.K. Nagar, Chennai – 600 078. Ph 044-24719947 / 48 / 49
Description In order to encourage the Hearing Impaired students to wear Hearing Aids for their Hearing potential thereby to increase their speech capacity. Behind the Ear Hearing Aids are provided for from 2011-12, as they hesitate to wear body level Hearing Aids.
