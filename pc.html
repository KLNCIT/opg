Welfare Of Differently Abled Persons-3 percent reservation of seats in educational institutions

3 percent of the total seats in Government and Government aided educational institutions have been allotted to the Differently Abled except in Medicine and Engineering courses. (1 percent each for Visually impaired persons, speech and hearing impaired and locomotor disabled.)

Appointment of guardians to special categories of differently abled persons under national trust act, 1999.

The persons affected with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, mental retardation and multiple disability who have crossed 18 years of age who are not in a position to take any decision. Hence under National Trust Act 1999 a Guardian can be appointed to look after their welfare, after the demise of their parents.
